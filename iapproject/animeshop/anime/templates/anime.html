Introduction

The website aims to provide a user-friendly interface, seamless shopping experience, and a diverse collection of high-quality anime-themed apparel for anime enthusiasts and fans worldwide. The primary objective of this project is to design and develop an engaging, visually appealing, and functional e-commerce platform specifically tailored for the sale of anime shirts. The website will feature a wide range of designs, sizes, and styles to cater to the diverse preferences of anime lovers and fashion enthusiasts.

Goals

  • CONSISTENT BRANDING
  • USER FRIENDLY NAVIGATION
  • ENGAGING HOME PAGE
  • VISUAL APPEAL IN SHOP PAGE
  • ANIME-INSPIRED VISUAL ELEMENTS

Target Audience

The target audience for the anime shirt e-commerce website includes:

  • Anime Fans: People who are passionate about anime culture, characters, and stories who seek unique and exclusive anime-themed apparel
  • Cosplayers: Fans who engage in cosplay activities and require specific anime-themed shirts to complement their costumes.
  • Collectors: People interested in collecting limited-edition or rare anime shirt designs as part of their merchandise collection.
  • Gift Shoppers: Individuals looking to purchase anime shirts as gifts for friends, family, or colleagues who share an interest in anime.
